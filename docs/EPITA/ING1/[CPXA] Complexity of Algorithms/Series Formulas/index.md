# Series Formulas

## Summary

$$

\sum_{i=a}^b S_i = \frac{(S_a+S_b)(b-a+1)}{2}\\
\sum_{i=1}^n i  = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}\\
$$

$$
\sum_{i=1}^ni^2=\frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}
$$

$$
\sum_{i=0}^nx^i = \frac{1-x^{n+1}}{1-x} (x\neq1)
\\
x = 1 \iff \sum_0^ni = n+1
$$
